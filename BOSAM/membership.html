Become A Member

The Constitution of the Baptist Old Students' Association spells out the conditions for membership of the Association as follows;

  • Any student who enrolled at the College and completed the prescribed certificate courses or spent at least one academic session shall be eligible for membership of the Association.
  • Any person, not being an Old Student of the College, who has shown sufficient interest in the affairs of the College and is adjudged to be of unimpeachable moral character, may be considered by the Executive Committee for admission as an Honorary Member of the Association.

If you have satisfied the above conditions, please click below to become a member or sign in as an already registered member.
