Welcome To BOSAM

History

Welcome to the official website of the Baptist Old Students' Association, Marke, Nigeria. This is an association of alumni of Baptist High School, Marke, Nigeria- One of the best secondary schools in Nasarawa State known for its excellent academic performances.The school was founded by the Baptist Convention, Kokona Local Government Area of Nasarawa State over a decade ago, and it commenced academic activity in September, 2005 at its temporary Site

Our Mission

1. To bring all past students together to provide a common forum to deliberate on matters concerning BOSAM.
2. To unite both past and present students of the school.
3. To provide a medium by which interest of the school shall be generated, promoted and sustained among the past students.
4. To act as Advisory body to the school.
5. To generate in the membership of the past students of B.H.S.M a selfless love among its members and their Alma Mater.
6. To have and maintain cordial relationship with other associations with similar interest.
7. To promote the intellectual, moral and physical development of the school.
